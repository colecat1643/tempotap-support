What to Include

To help us review your request, please briefly describe the issue type and the related page, feature, or asset.

  • Privacy questions, data handling requests, or permission-use concerns.
  • Asset source, licensing, attribution, or rights concerns involving images, videos, icons, audio, or other materials.
  • Factual errors, source attribution, or wording concerns in knowledge cards, collection entries, or historical content.
  • App issues, multiplayer problems, Mind Profile display or gameplay-data concerns, Energy display concerns, or other product feedback.

Helpful Details

For app issues, it may help to include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a brief description of what happened.

  • Please do not include unnecessary sensitive personal information in your message.
  • If you email us, we use your email address and message only to review and respond to your request.

FAQ and Fixes

Use these steps first if something does not work as expected.

  • If app content is missing or cannot load, update the app first. If it still fails, reinstall the app.
  • If a Daily Challenge or mini-game is unavailable, install the latest version and try again.
  • If room joining fails, keep devices nearby, check camera permission, and ask the host to create a new room QR code.
  • If a room becomes unstable, leave the room and create a new one instead of continuing the old session.

Safety Notes

TempoTap includes AR features that use the camera and nearby-device communication.

  • When using AR features, stay aware of your surroundings and play in a safe, open space.
  • TempoTap is a game experience, not a medical, diagnostic, or therapeutic product.
  • For multiplayer, keep devices nearby and use a reliable local network when available.
