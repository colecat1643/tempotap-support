Privacy Policy

TempoTap is designed around local play.

Local No ads or tracking No account required

What We Do Not Collect

No account is required to play.

  • We do not ask you to create an account.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • Nicknames, settings, Mind Profile data, Energy data, and game records are stored on your device.
  • We do not sell personal data, run ad tracking, or use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • We do not upload or save your camera feed or AR scan data.

Permissions

Permissions are used only for gameplay features.

  • Camera access is used to detect surfaces, and render AR game scenes. Camera frames are processed on device and do not leave your device.
  • The widget reads locally stored Energy data shared only between TempoTap and its widget on the same device.

Local Data

Game progress is stored locally.

  • Mind Profile, hand coordination, Endless Mode records, Energy level, language, display settings, and game progress are saved with on-device storage.
  • Your nickname is stored on device.
  • Your camera feed, AR scan data, Mind Profile, Energy data, game records, and local settings remain on your device and are not transmitted by the app.
  • You can reset Mind Profile and Energy data in Settings. Uninstalling the app also removes local app data from the device.

Third-Party Services

The app is designed without ads or tracking.

  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • The app does not send user data to AI services during gameplay.
  • If this changes, we will update this policy and our App Store privacy information.

Children's Privacy

The app is not designed to collect personal information from children.

  • TempoTap does not require children to create an account or provide a name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • If a parent or guardian believes that a child has contacted us or provided personal information by email, they may contact us and request deletion.

Your Choices

You can control or remove local game data.

  • You can reset Mind Profile and Energy data in Settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove local app data from your device.
  • If you contact us by email, you may request that we delete your support request information after we respond.

Other Notes

Additional notes about how the app works.

  • Some visual assets were created before release as original visual assets with AI-assisted tools. The app does not generate images with AI during gameplay and does not send user data to AI services.
  • Mind Profile and Energy are game-style progress indicators. They are not medical, diagnostic, treatment, or health advice.
  • For privacy questions or requests, please use Contact Support. If you email us, we use your email address and message only to respond to your request, and you may ask us to delete that request information.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to how TempoTap handles data, we will update this Privacy Policy and change the effective date above.
